Driver Email Help

The driver email feature lets you send emails to the drivers directly from the application.

Fields:

  • To is the drivers name for your information.
  • Email shows the driver's email or "No Email Defined." In the latter case, you may type an email address in the field.
  • Subject is the message subject.
  • Message is the body of the email.

Email, subject and message are all required. You must also have your email defined in the user table.

Click the "Send Email" button to send it. It will return an alert if it needs anything. It will return an alert letting you know if the message went out ok or not.
